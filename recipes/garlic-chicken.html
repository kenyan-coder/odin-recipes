Garlic Chicken Recipe

Description

This is a simple DIY for garlic chicken. Do it at home and enjoy the taste of a 7 Star.Yum!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves of crashed garlic
  • 1/4 cup Italian-saesoned bread crumbs
  • 4 chicken breasts, halved and skinless
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients and preheat the oven at 220 degrees C
  2. Heat olive oil and garlic in small sauce pan under medium heat for 1 to 2 minutes. Pour it a shallow bowl
  3. Combine bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese in a separate shallow bowl
  4. Use tongs to dip chicken breasts in olive oil-garlic mizture then coat evenly in bread crumb mixture
  5. Transfer the coated chicken to a shallow baking dish
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes. Make sure the juices run clear and breasts no longer pink. An instant read thermometer should read 75 degrees C

Final Notes

Server while moderately warm to enjoy the full nutritional benefits and the yummy breasts
